# Product Guidelines

## Prose Style
The overall tone of the user interface and documentation should be **Approachable & Warm**. While the underlying technology is complex, the user-facing language should be friendly, welcoming, and accessible to everyone. Avoid overly technical jargon where possible.

## Branding
Sana's brand identity is **High-Performance & Modern**. We emphasize speed, efficiency, and the use of cutting-edge technology (Rust, Wasm, NATS) to deliver a superior messaging experience. The visual language should be clean, professional, and forward-looking.

## UX Principles
The core UX principle for Sana is **Speed & Responsiveness**.
- **Instant Feedback:** Every user interaction (sending a message, switching channels) must feel immediate.
- **Low Latency:** Optimize for the fastest possible data transfer and rendering.
- **Fluid UI:** Ensure smooth transitions and animations that reinforce the sense of speed.

## Error Handling
Our approach to error handling is **Silent & Seamless**.
- **Minimize Disruption:** The app should handle minor errors and temporary connection issues gracefully in the background without interrupting the user's flow.
- **Proactive Recovery:** Implement automatic retries and fallback mechanisms to resolve issues silently.
- **Minimal Notifications:** Only notify the user of critical errors that require their direct intervention, using clear and helpful language.
